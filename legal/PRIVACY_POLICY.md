## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our mobile application, websites, and related services that link to this policy.

## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 You provide

- **Account data:** email, password (we store credentials via our authentication provider; we do not store your raw password in readable form), display name, profile details you choose to add.
- **Support & communications:** messages you send us, including email or in-app support requests.
- **Payment-related data:** when you add funds or receive payouts, **payment processing is handled by third parties** (e.g., Stripe). We receive limited information necessary to operate your account (e.g., transaction status, identifiers our processor associates with you—not your full card number stored on our servers in most cases).

### 2.2 Collected automatically

- **Device & app data:** device type, operating system, app version, language, general diagnostic logs.
- **Usage data:** features used, gameplay and contest-related data needed to run matches, leaderboards, and integrity checks.
- **Identifiers:** device or advertising identifiers where applicable and permitted by your OS settings and this policy.
- **Approximate location:** if you provide a region or we derive coarse location for compliance (e.g., eligibility for certain features), as described at collection or in account settings.

### 2.3 From third parties

- **Authentication & fraud:** our vendors may provide signals to help secure accounts.
- **Payment processors:** confirmation of payments, refunds, or payout status as needed to credit or debit your account in accordance with our terms.

We do **not** knowingly collect sensitive categories (e.g., health, biometric templates used for identification) unless we specifically ask and explain why.
